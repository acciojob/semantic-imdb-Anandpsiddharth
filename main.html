Summary

Pathaan is a 2023 Indian action thriller directed by Siddharth Anand. The story follows an Indian spy, Pathaan, who comes out of exile to stop a terrorist organization from attacking India. Filled with high-octane action scenes, emotions, and patriotism, the movie showcases courage, loyalty, and the spirit of sacrifice for the nation.

Actors

  • Shah Rukh Khan as Pathaan
  • Deepika Padukone as Rubina Mohsin
  • John Abraham as Jim
  • Dimple Kapadia as Nandini Grewal
  • Ashutosh Rana as Colonel Luthra
