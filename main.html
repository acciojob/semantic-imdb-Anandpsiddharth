Summary

Pathaan is a 2023 Indian action thriller film directed by Siddharth Anand. The story follows a fearless Indian spy, Pathaan, who comes out of exile to stop a deadly terror organization from attacking India. The movie combines intense action, patriotism, and emotional depth, showcasing how one man risks everything to protect his country.

Actors

  • Shah Rukh Khan as Pathaan
  • Deepika Padukone as Rubina Mohsin
  • John Abraham as Jim
  • Dimple Kapadia as Nandini Grewal
  • Ashutosh Rana as Colonel Luthra
